Ambiance & Noise generator app.
About app

This app allows you to generate several sounds that are helpful for focus, relaxation, sleep, blocking external sounds, and etc.

It has several ambiances and can generate white, pink or brown noises.

There are several presets with most common sounds people seem to love, but there is an advanced section which allows you to configure the sounds to your requirement.
